Emerging Sports Professionals (ESP) is an organization that connects the leaders of tomorrow with today’s industry thought leaders to share best practices and discuss innovative trends in sports & entertainment.

Currently, ESP is exclusively located in New York City and Chicago under the chapter names of Emerging New York Sports Professionals and Emerging Chicago Sports Professionals.
